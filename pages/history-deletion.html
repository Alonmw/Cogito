Chat History Deletion - Cogito

Delete Your Conversation History

You have full control over your chat and conversation history in Cogito. This page explains how to delete your chat history while keeping your account active.

ℹ️ What This Covers

This page specifically addresses the deletion of your chat history and conversation data. If you want to delete your entire account, please visit our Account Deletion page.

Types of History Data

Your Cogito app stores the following types of conversation data:

  • Chat Messages: All messages you've sent and received
  • Conversation Threads: Individual conversation sessions
  • Socratic Dialogue History: Records of philosophical discussions
  • Question and Answer Pairs: Your Q&A interaction history
  • Session Metadata: Timestamps and conversation context

History Deletion Options

Option 1: Delete Individual Conversations

Step 1: Open the Cogito app
Step 2: Navigate to your chat or conversation list
Step 3: Long-press or swipe on a specific conversation
Step 4: Select "Delete" or "Remove"
Step 5: Confirm the deletion when prompted

Option 2: Clear All Chat History

Step 1: Open the Cogito app
Step 2: Go to Settings or Profile
Step 3: Look for "Privacy & Data" or "Chat Settings"
Step 4: Select "Clear All History" or "Delete All Conversations"
Step 5: Confirm by typing "DELETE" or following the verification process

Option 3: Automatic History Deletion

Step 1: Open the Cogito app
Step 2: Go to Settings → Privacy
Step 3: Find "Auto-delete History" settings
Step 4: Choose your preferred timeframe (e.g., 30 days, 90 days, 1 year)
Step 5: Save your settings

This will automatically delete conversations older than your selected timeframe.

Option 4: Request History Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app or need assistance:

Send an email to: support@cogito-app.com
Subject: Chat History Deletion Request
Include: Your registered email, account details, and specific deletion request
Specify: Whether you want to delete all history or specific conversations
Response time: We will process your request within 5 business days

⚠️ Important Considerations

  • Permanent Action: Deleted conversations cannot be recovered
  • Local vs Cloud: History is deleted from both your device and our servers
  • Backup Reminder: Export important conversations before deletion if needed
  • Account Remains Active: Your account stays active, only history is deleted

What Happens After Deletion

Once you delete your chat history:

  • Conversations are immediately removed from your app interface
  • Data is permanently deleted from our servers within 24-48 hours
  • Backup systems are purged within 30 days
  • Your account settings and preferences remain unchanged
  • You can start new conversations immediately

Data Export Before Deletion

If you want to keep a copy of your conversations:

  • Use the in-app export feature (if available)
  • Take screenshots of important conversations
  • Request a data export via email: support@cogito-app.com
  • Copy important text to your device's notes app

Privacy and Security

Your privacy is important to us:

  • Deleted data is permanently removed and cannot be accessed by our team
  • We use secure deletion methods to ensure data cannot be recovered
  • No deleted conversations are used for analytics or training purposes
  • Deletion logs are maintained for compliance but contain no personal content
