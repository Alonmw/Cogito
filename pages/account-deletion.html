Account Deletion - Cogito

Delete Your Account

We understand that you may want to delete your Cogito account. This page provides information about the account deletion process and what it means for your data.

⚠️ Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once you delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information and account details
  • All chat history and conversation data
  • App usage analytics associated with your account
  • Any personalized settings and preferences
  • All data stored in connection with your account

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: In-App Deletion

Step 1: Open the Cogito app on your device
Step 2: Go to Settings or Profile section
Step 3: Look for "Delete Account" or "Account Settings"
Step 4: Follow the prompts to confirm account deletion
Step 5: Enter your password or confirm your identity
Step 6: Confirm the deletion when prompted

Option 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app or need assistance with account deletion, you can contact us directly:

Send an email to: support@cogito-app.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request
Include: Your registered email address and reason for deletion
Response time: We will process your request within 30 days

Data Retention After Deletion

After account deletion:

  • Most data is deleted immediately
  • Some backup data may take up to 90 days to be completely purged from all systems
  • We may retain certain information for legal compliance or fraud prevention as required by law
  • Anonymous usage statistics (not linked to your identity) may be retained for analytics purposes

Alternative Options

Before deleting your account, consider these alternatives:

  • Data Export: Request a copy of your data before deletion
  • Temporary Deactivation: Contact us about temporarily deactivating your account
  • Privacy Settings: Adjust your privacy settings instead of full deletion

Need Help?

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance, please contact us:

Email: support@cogito-app.com

Response Time: 1-3 business days

We're here to help and ensure your data rights are respected.

Confirmation

Once your account is deleted, you will receive a confirmation email at your registered email address. This confirms that your account and associated data have been successfully removed from our systems.
